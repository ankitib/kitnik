How To Reach Kanpur

From Singapore

You can fly in to Delhi (DEL),Mumbai (BOM),Kolkata (CCU) then take a flight to Kanpur (KNU).

Alternatively you can fly in direct (Scoot) to Lucknow (LKO), then take a cab to Kanpur.The car ride is approximately 2 hours.(80 km)

In India

Kanpur (KNU) has direct flights (Spicejet) from Delhi (DEL),Mumbai (BOM),Kolkata (CCU).

There are many trains running from all major railway stations to Kanpur (CNB)

Alternatively you can fly in direct to Lucknow (LKO), then take a cab to Kanpur.The cab ride is approximately 2 hours.(80 km)
